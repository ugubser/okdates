OkDates

Find the perfect date that works for everyone - use natural language, instead of clicking buttons and selecting dates

OkDates is a free tool, that doesn't require any registration and is completely anonymous

How it works

1

Create an event or a meeting

Generate a unique shareable link for your event, set a password or store the administrator link securely

2

Share with friends

Send the link to all participants or add them yourself one by one

3

Collect availability

Everyone enters their available dates in free text or you do it for them

4

Find the best date

Agree on the best availability for everyone, download the ical calendar invite (as Administrator) and send to everyone
